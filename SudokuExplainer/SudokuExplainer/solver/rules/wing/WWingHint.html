W-Wing

The two cells {2} and {3} have a strong link on digit {5} (the x value).

A strong link between 2 candidates exists if at least one of them must be true.

If one of the cells {2} or {3} must be a digit {5} then either cell {0} or cell {1} cannot be a digit {5} but must contain a digit {4} (the w value).
